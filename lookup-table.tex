        \(\left\lvert\Delta x\right\rvert \gg 0\)일 때, 
        \[\lg\left(x+\Delta x\right) = \lg\left(x\right) + \lg{e}\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{\frac{\left(-1\right)^{n+1}}{n}\left(\frac{\Delta x}{x}\right)^{n}}\]
